ReConnect: A Weekend Marriage & Pickleball Retreat

Communication, commitment, connection, and pickleball. Spend an unforgettable weekend reconnecting in your marriage and growing your pickleball skills.

View Pricing Ask a Question
For Couples

A Weekend To Reconnect And Play

Couples can choose the full retreat with the marriage workshop, dinner, and pickleball clinics, or attend the workshop and dinner only.

Marriage Workshop

Led By Owen Mitchell

Senior National Pickleball Champion and marriage expert Owen Mitchell leads this two-day experience to strengthen your marriage and help you grow closer together spiritually, emotionally, and physically.

The workshop includes engaging teaching, short videos, laughter, small-group moments, and powerful couple-to-couple conversation. Owen and his wife have been doing these workshops for 26 years with thousands of couples.

Pickleball Clinic

Led By Scott Moore

Hall of Famer and 21-time national senior champion Scott Moore leads the clinic experience designed to take your pickleball to the next level.

Scott will be joined by senior pro Bill Muno and Senior National Pickleball Champion and marriage expert Owen Mitchell.

Included

Workshop, Dinner, Clinic, And Play

ReConnect Workshop

Friday evening and Saturday morning sessions led by Owen Mitchell and hosted at Outreach Inc. headquarters.

5550 Tech Center Dr, Colorado Springs

Gourmet Catered Dinner

Baron of beef, oven roasted chipotle salmon, herb roasted potatoes, grilled asparagus, tossed salad, rolls, drinks, and dessert.

Clinic & Play With A Pro

Two hours of clinic time followed by 90 minutes of play with a pro to practice what you learned. Players will be divided into groups by skill level.
