Meet Our Instructors

Private lessons are $60/hr for up to two people with court time included. Clinics and programs are available for every level, from first-time players to advanced competitors.

Player Development

Lessons Built Around Your Game

Work on fundamentals, strategy, shot selection, movement, match play, or tournament preparation. Many lessons include video so you can review what changed and keep improving after you leave the court.

Head Pro and Top 100 PPA Pro · West

Miles Hoover

With a lifelong background in competitive tennis and more than two years at Springs Pickleball, Miles brings energy, focus, and high-level training to private lessons and group clinics.

He competes and trains with top players, then brings that tour-level learning back to the players he coaches.

Ellen Steinlein

Graham grew up in London with a passion for golf and tennis, then became a teaching professional in Jacksonville before discovering pickleball in Colorado Springs.

His lessons use strategic, purposeful drills to help players of all levels build better decisions and cleaner execution.

Susie Roberts

Susie helps players move beyond intro-level pickleball and refine the details that matter: net transition, shot selection, mechanics, partner communication, resets, dinks, blocks, and drops.

She brings six years of playing experience and a welcoming, community-first coaching style.
