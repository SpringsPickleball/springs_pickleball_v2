The ROI Math

The ROI math

Let's be honest about what Colorado Springs advertising costs.

Reaching 100-300 qualified, health-conscious local residents through paid Facebook or Google ads runs $500-$2,000+ per campaign. Passive impressions. No relationship. No guarantee they're even your customer.

With Springs Pickleball you get:

  • Face-to-face access to your exact target - 100-300 per event
  • The same audience seeing your brand week after week
  • Digital amplification (social, email, livestream) stacked on top
  • A relationship - not an ad

Event Table Bronze: $250

= $0.83-$2.50 per attendee reached, in person.

Compare to $5-$15 per click on digital, with zero conversation.

Court Partner: $1,000/month

Net brand-exposure cost: ~$400/month for daily exposure to every player who walks in.
